💡 Conclusion

Sequential fine-tuning is effective for teaching structured output generation.

While a small amount of forgetting occurs after Stage 2 training, the model successfully balances:

  • ✅ General instruction-following capabilities
  • ✅ Structured JSON output generation
  • ✅ Trade-off optimization through hyperparameter tuning

This demonstrates that sequential fine-tuning can effectively balance specialized learning and generalization when carefully tuned, with no catastrophic forgetting observed.
